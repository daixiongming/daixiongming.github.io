About Me

I got my Ph.D. from Division of Computer Science and Engineering, School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Louisiana State University. I was a director for motion capture system development at Shenzhen Realis Multimedia Technology Co., Ltd.. I am an associate professor at Guangzhou Institute of Science and Technology. Simultaneously, I am working as a CTO and chief AI scientist for a semiconductor company in Shanghai, China.
